ABOUT ARENANGOR

Building The Future Of Sports Communities

Arenangor is more than just a sports venue. We are a hub where students, athletes, and communities connect through sports.

OUR STORY

Started From A Simple Problem

Students often struggle to find available sports facilities, compare prices, and manage bookings efficiently. Arenangor was created to solve this problem by providing a centralized sports facility platform.

Vision

To become the leading sports hub platform for students and communities in Indonesia.

Mission

Make sports facilities more accessible, affordable, and connected.
